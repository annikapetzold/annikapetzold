Pocket Park on a Supermarket in Rio de Janeiro

H.M. Pauli, A. Petzold  ·  2025  ·  Prof. Dr. Luiz Felipe da Cunha, UFRJ

Infrastructure

Strengthen the repopulation of vacant buildings

Park

Improve urban quality and build a social catalyst

Urban Connection

Structure based on Rio's expressive architectural language

Rio's Centro suffers from vacancy: empty buildings, absent residents, and declining public life. Rather than adding to the built mass, the project repurposes a parking lot into a layered intervention with a supermarket as neighbourhood infrastructure, topped by a park as ecological and social catalyst. Ramped pathways stitch street to pedestrian alley, while the raw concrete structure echoes Brazilian brutalism and the organic forms of the park above mirror the tropical abundant nature. The project is designed as a first step: activating the ground to attract investment into the surrounding vacant buildings.
