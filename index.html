Bio

Architecture student at the Technische Universität München, currently on exchange at the Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro. My work moves between the rural and the urban, the technical and the spatial — from hemp-built community structures in Franconia to performing arts schools in Munich and pocket parks above parking lots in Rio de Janeiro.

Before architecture, I studied Economics & Business Administration at Fernuniversität Hagen, a background that informs how I think about architecture as infrastructure — social, ecological, and economic. I am drawn to projects where building becomes a catalyst for something larger than itself.
